Embed FlippingBook interactive publications into your blog post or WordPress webpage quickly and easily.

== Description ==

This plugin allows you to embed publications created with FlippingBook Publisher and FlippingBook Online in one step without tweaking any of the embed code.
 
Once the plugin is installed, you can copy and paste the URL of your FlippingBook publication to the body of your page or post. The publication will be added immediately, giving you a colorful, interactive way to engage your audience.

You can see examples of interactive publications at our [website](http://flippingbook.com/presentation-examples).

== Installation ==

Here’s how to make the plugin work for your publications.

1. Download and activate the plugin through the Plugins menu in WordPress admin.
2. Copy the FlippingBook publication’s URL from your browser and paste it into the body of your post. For testing purposes, you can use our demo publication here: [https://flippingbook.cld.bz/htf-magazine](https://flippingbook.cld.bz/htf-magazine). Just embed it into your page and click on the “Preview” button to see how it looks.
3. To create your own FlippingBook publications, you can use [one of our services](https://flippingbook.com/).
